Purpose In the last few years, Artificial Intelligence (AI) keeps coming to the attention of researchers. However, more and more AI models are recognized to be "black box" models because of their opaque, complex internal structure. To explain the model performance and build user trust, we propose an interactive tool whose dynamic analysis can help the user better gain knowledge of the model-internal reasoning process. Our interactive tool exploits the state-of-the-art deep neural language model. The proposed method first extracts the information of essential layers and heads from the AI model. Then our model summarizes information as the similarity score. Based on the similarity score, our model will retrieve the ranking list. Finally, Our specific explanation visualizes the internal process of the model in a manner that explains to the user “Why does this particular input lead to that particular output". We are expecting our proposed application will help the user understand the relationship of input and output of the AI model and how the AI model arrived with the resulting decisions on the input features.The study is an exploration to determine the correctness of the resume classification system result and to evaluate its interpretability and veracity among different users.

Study Design The principal investigator will analyze the application mistakes that the participants find during the experiments in order to design a better interface for aiding users to study the underlying relationship of input and output of the AI model. The principal investigator will also analyze the participant’s feedback, operation process and questionnaires.
